Our story

Dallas’s Home Cleaning Specialists

To provide exceptional, eco-friendly cleaning services that make every home shine while respecting the environment.

Reyes Maids was founded by Monarca Home Services with a simple mission: deliver reliable, high-quality house cleaning that Dallas families can count on. We started with a small team and a commitment to using safe, effective products. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted name in home cleaning, serving hundreds of satisfied customers across the city.

Our team is trained, insured, and passionate about cleanliness. We believe that a clean home creates peace of mind, and we take pride in being part of our clients’ lives. Whether it’s a weekly touch-up or a deep clean before a special occasion, we treat every home with the same care and attention we would our own.
