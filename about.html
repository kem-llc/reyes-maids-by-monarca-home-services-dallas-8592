Our story

About Reyes Maids – Your Local Cleaning Partner

To provide Dallas families with a healthier, cleaner home through reliable, eco-friendly cleaning services that exceed expectations.

Reyes Maids was founded in 2014 by Maria Reyes, a Dallas native who saw the need for trustworthy, high-quality house cleaning in her community. Starting with just a bucket and a dream, she built a team of dedicated professionals who share her passion for cleanliness and customer care. Today, Reyes Maids is part of Monarca Home Services, serving hundreds of homes across Dallas with the same personal touch that started it all.

We believe that a clean home is the foundation of a happy life. That’s why we invest in training, eco-friendly products, and a satisfaction guarantee. Every cleaner is bonded, insured, and background-checked – because your trust is our most valuable asset.
